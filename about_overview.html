About Blue Leaf

Blue Leaf is a socially-conscious cosmetics and self-care company dedicated to providing affordable, environmentally friendly products to consumers. The problem with eco-friendly products is that they’re extremely expensive, and are also a hassle to find since many are falsely advertised as being green. The solution we provide is a box sent directly to your doorstep with all kinds of environmentally-friendly shampoos, lotions, and soaps, among other things. By subscribing to the Blue Leaf box, you can cut down on the hassle and the prices in your search for green products.
